About Tom Willford

Law Is In
His Blood.

Tom Willford didn't stumble into law — he was born into it. His father and uncle founded Willford & Willford in the 1950s. His cousin practiced for 40 years in Whittier. For Tom, this isn't a career. It's a calling passed down through generations.

With 32 years of litigation across state and federal courts, Tom has seen every tactic insurance companies use to minimize your claim. He knows exactly how to stop them — because he's a rider himself who understands what it feels like to be on the road.
